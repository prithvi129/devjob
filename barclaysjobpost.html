About Structured Credit Trading Team:

The Structured Credit trading team at Barclays focuses on trading bespoke structured products and structuring specific solutions for clients in the form of providing financing or creating customized products. Using underlying credit instruments including Corporate, Financial & Sovereign bonds, single name CDS and Loans, the team aims to create products that provide leverage or cater to the specific needs of a client. Within Structured Credit Trading, the business is split as liability (or financing) side business and asset side business. The Liability side business focuses on providing bespoke financing solutions to credit funds, FIs and banks against wide variety of credit assets – both on a recourse and non-recourse basis. The traded products are in the form of Repos, Loans and TRS. The Asset side business specializes in providing credit linked investment products to investors for yield enhancement. Typical traded products include vanilla and structured CLNs, repacks of liquid and illiquid securities and leveraged note instruments.

About Structured Credit Trading Team – Mumbai

Structured Credit team in Mumbai works with trading & analyst teams across the globe and supports the traders and senior management by owning end to end Risk Management including managing the funding risk, capital management, exposure management, overseeing margin call process, pipeline and in-depth risk & PnL analysis. The team also produces business critical MIS reports, builds and maintains tools to help find trading opportunities and recommend trade ideas, analyze companies, track news and produce other customized analytics.
