Salary : INR 4.01 LPA

Industry : IT Industry

Profile/Designation: Software Engineer Trainee

Visit Company Website : Mount Blue.com

Company- Mount Blue Technologies Private Limited

MountBlue is a high-end supplier of software development services. We closely collaborate with small and medium-sized businesses and startups to launch their goods. A company offering premium software development services is one that teaches you to code and then deploys. To market their products, they collaborate closely with small and medium-sized businesses and startups. They realize their customers' vision by utilizing popular open-source technologies and agile software development approach. Since they only offer exclusive services, they state that "we choose our customers, not our customers choose us."

Eligibility Criteria for Mount Blue Off Campus Drive 2023:

  • BE/ B.Tech/ M.Tech/ MCA from 2023 batch
  • pH score Mandatory
  • College should allow immediate joining as interns â€“ the internship will be in premise and not remote

Job Description:

  • MountBlue is looking for smart and motivated engineers to join their team. The candidate must have programming proficiency in at least one of the following languages: C, C++, Javascript, Java, Python, PHP or Ruby.
  • Selected candidates will go through an extensive 12 weeks training program and on successfully completing the requirements of the program will be offered the role of a Software Development Engineer.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Work in a team to develop, compile, test, debug & deploy software
  • Develop web and mobile applications using Javascript, Java, Python, HTML, CSS etc
  • Ensure close to 100% timely & defect-free software deployments
  • Write documentation for the software and APIs
  • Support software during production and provide hot fixes

Requirements:

  • Familiarity with at least one programming language. E.g. C, C++, Javascript, Java, PHP or Python
  • Good analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Self-motivated with the ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Eagerness to learn new technologies

Benefits:

  • You will get the opportunity to interact with and learn from the CTOs and Directors of Engineering of leading technology startups in India
  • Perfect launchpad for a high flying career in technology

How to Apply for Mount Blue Off Campus Drive 2023 ?

  • Candidates need to take PH Test in order to apply for the posts of Software Engineer Trainee. Candidates should apply on or before 5th August 2023. Shortlisted candidates can attend the drive on 6th August 2023.
