HCLTech NSDC Industry Internship Program 2025


Overview:

Software engineers (SWEs) work with teammates to solve problems and build innovative software solutions. You are passionate about customers and product quality, and you provide technical guidance to Program Managers as they consider user needs and product requirements. You will also be expected to demonstrate an ability to learn and adopt relevant new technologies, tools, methods and processes to leverage in your solutions.


HCLTechnologies

HCLTech, officially known as HCL Technologies Limited, is a leading Indian multinational IT services and consulting company headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. Established on August 11, 1976, by Shiv Nadar, HCL began as Hindustan Computers Limited, focusing initially on hardware. Over the years, it transitioned into a global IT powerhouse, offering a wide range of services and products.

Eligibility:

    Educational Background: Students pursuing or who have completed degrees in B.Tech, B.E, MCA, M.Sc (IT), BCA, or related fields. Graduates from Commerce, Business, and Arts backgrounds can apply for non-technical roles..

Job Responsibilities:

  • Subject Matter Expert-Led Training: Attend virtual sessions conducted by industry experts to gain insights into current industry practices and technologies.
  • Capstone Projects: Work on real-world projects that simulate industry challenges, allowing interns to apply their knowledge and develop practical solutions.
  • Skill Development: Enhance both technical and soft skills through structured learning modules and hands-on experiences.
  • Collaborative Learning: Engage with peers and mentors in collaborative environments to foster teamwork and knowledge sharing.
  • Exposure to Industry Tools: Gain familiarity with tools and technologies prevalent in the IT industry, preparing for future employment opportunities.

Experience: Freshers

Designation: Intern[Virtual]

Industry: IT Industry

Skill required: Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively in a team environment.
