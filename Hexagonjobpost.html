Company - Hexagon PPM

The PPM division of Hexagon, a leader in sensor, software, and autonomous solutions, is a part of Hexagon. In industrial, manufacturing, infrastructure, safety, and mobility applications, we are using data to increase efficiency, productivity, and quality. In order to ensure a scalable, sustainable future, our technologies are transforming urban and production ecosystems to become more linked and autonomous. The PPM business of Hexagon gives its customers the tools to turn unstructured data into smart digital assets that can be used to visualize, construct, and manage structures and facilities of any complexity, ensuring their safe and effective operation throughout the course of their entire lifecycle. Our worldwide headquarters in Madison, Alabama, USA, is a five-story, 250,000-square-foot structure with office space for 1,100 Hexagon staff members. It was formally launched in October 2014. Additionally, the corporate headquarters building houses a cutting-edge data center, conference.

Eligibility Criteria for Hexagon Recruitment 2023:

  • Bachelor of Engineering.
  • Should have working knowledge of 0 to 2 years of experience with Java or C++ and API testing.
  • Should be a team player, with good communication, and interpersonal skills, working with overseas counterparts.

Job Description:

As a Quality Assurance Analyst

  • Write the test plans for all the new implementations and for release testing workflows.
  • Manually execute the test plans and also downstream workflows for frequent releases.
  • Need to support in manual testing of new implementations and technical debt.
  • Should be involved with the development (as a quality advocate) from the design phase/coding phase to ensure the Testability of the code.
  • Should support in the deployments of build-in CI/CD pipeline.
  • Should ensure maximum automation coverage of test cases to drive for Shift-Left Methodology.
  • Write ATPs for the test cases/workflows provided by the Functional Engineer (FE) for the implementation in the product.
  • When writing the ATPs for the product use the in-house framework or collaborate with the team to create a framework.
  • Work closely with Functional Engineer (FE) on validating the feasibility of test scenarios for automation.
  • Should be able to understand the Product Code.
  • Report the unit tests and ATPs coverage.
  • Work closely with the Scrum team and QA members to ensure ATPs are in place for the implementation being done in the product.
  • Understand and Follow the QA process (Scrum) and ensure minimal bugs/issues are reported by the QA once the ATPs creation/execution is completed.
  • Collaborate with the Manager on prioritizing the Automation backlog.
