Molex Recruitment 2023 | Freshers | Graduate Engineer Trainee | BE/ B.Tech – Mechanical Engineering | Bangalore


Experience: Freshers

Designation: Graduate Engineer Trainee

Industry: Engineering

Job Location: Bangalore

Company Website: Molex.com

Company Molex:

Leading global provider of cutting-edge electrical components and solutions is Molex. We create and provide solutions every day to simplify your life. You can always find our innovation, whether it's in the newest smartphone, a reliable home appliance, or a linked car. We have been in business for nearly 75 years and are proud of our many inventions and the technology they have made possible. The first mobile phone, the first HDTV, and the first car radio all benefited from the use of Molex. One thing all of our ground-breaking solutions have in common is that they assist top technology businesses in realizing their own breakthroughs.

Eligibility Criteria for Molex Recruitment 2023:

  • BE/ B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering.

Job Description:

  • You will be a part of PSBU division for Engineering at Molex. You will be reporting to Sr. Engineer – Product Engineering

Job Responsibilities:

  • Understand Customer Drawing and Specification
  • Create 3D models based on Concept.
  • Prepare Molex Drawings and release in system.
  • Prepare solid models & drawings from Concept.
  • Release and Update drawings and specifications to Work Manager
  • Provide Bill of Material for costing & assist in updating in SAP updation.
  • Participate in product concepts and proposal development.
  • Initiate engineering change via ECR/ECN & PCN and monitor progress till it gets released in System.
  • Work with other design center to get clarifications on design / material etc. for products manufactured but not controlled by Molex India (cable harness).

Job Requirements:

  • Good skills in NX, Pro E software’s.
  • Good and effective technical communication skills.

Skills:

  • Good skills in Product Design and Development
  • Fare skills in NX, Pro E OSDD, OSM, AutoCAD and E-EC
  • Fare knowledge of Product design and development processes.
  • Moderate technical communication skills
  • Moderate Product knowledge
  • Knowledge on wiring harness manufacturing process will be an added advantage
