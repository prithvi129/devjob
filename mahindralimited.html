B.E/B.Tech For Off-Campus Freshers for 2023 As Graduate Apprentice Trainee In Bangalore


Eligibility Criteria: B.Tech/B.E (Mechanical / Production)

Work Experience: 0 - 1 Years

Profile/Designation: Graduate Apprentice Trainee

Industry: Electrical, Engineering

Job Description

About the company:

Mahindra and Mahindra Restricted is an Indian worldwide car producing company settled in Mumbai. It was laid out in 1945 as Mahindra and Mohammed and later renamed as Mahindra and Mahindra. A piece of the Mahindra Gathering, M&M is one of the biggest vehicle makers by creation in India.

Compamny Website: Mahindra.com

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Planning & execution to Achieve Daily Targets.
  • Resource management and Operator training.
  • Resolution of Buy-off concern.
  • Updation of SOS/process procedure, Stagewise audit, Implement CAPA and Production GAP analysis.
  • Analysis and action plan for reduction of defects and Process rejection.
  • Conversion cost reduction.

Skill Set Required:

  • Planning and execution skills.
  • Analytical Skils.
  • Problem solving skills.
  • Functional knowledge of Electrical system / Automobile system.
  • Resource management ,Team building ,Training and Development of operators.
  • Line balancing and single piece flow set up.
  • Knowledge on Product and Industrial safety.
  • Operator skill development and mutlitasking.

Job location: Bangalore

Candidate location: Bangalore

Desired experience: 0-1 Years of experience
