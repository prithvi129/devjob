Wipro Job Openings for Freshers as Trainee in Pan India


Experience: Freshers (0 Years)

Salary: INR 15,488.00 to INR 23,000.00 per Month

Designation: Trainee

Industry: IT Industry

Job Location: Pan India

Company Website: Wipro.com

Company Wipro:

Wipro is the leading strategic IT partner for companies across India, the Middle East and Asia–Pacific – offering integrated IT solutions. They plan, deploy, sustain and maintain your IT lifecycle through their total outsourcing, consulting services, business solutions and professional services.

Eligibility Criteria for Wipro Recruitment 2023:

  • Eligibility for this pooled Off Campus is BCA & BSc
  • Applicants should be only from 2021, 2022 or 2023 batch pass out
  • Freshers should have minimum of 60% aggregate marks in graduation
  • All arrears and backlogs need to be cleared at the time of selection process
  • Maximum of 3 years of education gap, if any, is allowed between 10th and graduation
  • No Gaps are allowed in Graduation. Graduation should be completed within 3 years
  • Education should be regular full time only in 10th, 12th & Graduation recognized by the Central/State Government of India
  • On joining, candidates would be required to sign a service agreement for 36 months

Wipro Freshers Recruitment - Interview Process:

  • Online Assessment (80 minutes)
  • Upon selection in the Online Assessment, candidates will be required to go through a Technical Interview
  • HR Interview
  • Interested and eligible candidates should apply as soon as possible before link gets expired. Shortlisted candidates will get interview call letter
