Mindfire Job Openings for freshers as Associate Software Engineer - Remote Working


Experience: Fresher (0 to 1 years)

Designation: Associate Software Engineer

Industry: IT Industry

Job Location: Bhubaneswar, Delhi OR Remote Working

Company Website: Mindfiresolutions.com

Company Mindfire Solutions:

Mindfire Solutions is amongst the leading providers of Software & IT services encompassing development and delivery of complex projects for enhancing business growth of its customers. Mindfire Solutions is a 20 years old software development & IT services company.

Eligibility Criteria for Mindfire Solutions Recruitment 2023:

  • Educational qualification for this job opening is BE /BTech/ ME/ MTech in Computer Science or IT
  • Freshers from 2021, 2022 & 2023 batch pass out can apply
  • Good academic records are added as advantage
  • Exposure to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, ReactJS, Angular
  • Good to have knowledge of backend technologies, i.e. Python, Java, .NET, Node.js
  • Exposure to SQL (MSSQL, PostgreSQL, MySQL or Similar) / NoSQL (MongoDB or Similar)
  • Exposure to Git - GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket or similar SCM tools
  • Preferably with 6 months Internship done
  • Open to learning-related technologies

Job Description:

  • Work with product owners, architects, developers, testers and other stakeholders in the client's organization and in Mindfire to build and maintain software solutions
  • Get specifications/wireframes/ mock-ups and translate them into scalable, high performance, robust web apps!
  • Responsible to write and maintaining clean, well-written code, as per the best practices
  • Open to learning-related technologies

Mindfire Freshers Recruitment 2023 - Interview Process:

  • Online Written Test
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview
